Extended Alphabet

Add extra characters to the alphabet recognized by Wordology. For example, if you add the hyphen ("-"), then Wordology will consider "flower-pot" as one word rather than "flower" and "pot". Just type all extra characters together, with no spaces, eg '- to add ' and -.
